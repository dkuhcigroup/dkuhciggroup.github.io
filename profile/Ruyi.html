Ruyi Li

Ruyi Li is a PhD candidate at HKUST (Guangzhou) under Prof. Dr. Xin Tong, specializing in Extended Reality (XR), collaboration, and generative AI. She previously worked as a research assistant at the Institute for AI Industry Research at Tsinghua University, where she collaborated with Prof. Jiangtao Gong. Ruyi's research focuses on creating innovative prototypes, such as the augmented reality telepresence robot prototype for collaborative locomotion.
